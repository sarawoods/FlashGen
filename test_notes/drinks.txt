Drink Recipes

~ Bahama Mama
> 1 oz Rum (White)
> 1 oz Rum (Dark)
> 1 oz Rum (Spiced)
> Sweet & Sour
> Orange Mix
> Pineapple Juice
> Dash of Grenadine
Pour all the rums into a glass with ice.
Fill the glass with equal parts of the juices.
Add the dash of grenadine on top.

~ Black Russian
> 1 1/2 oz vodka
> 1 oz coffee liqueur
Pour vodka and coffee liqueur into a glass with ice.

~ Cadillac Margarita
> 1 1/2 oz Premium Tequila
> 1/2 oz Cointreau Orange Liqueur
> Sweet & Sour or Margarita Mix
> Grand Marnier
Rub rim of a margarita glass with a lime and dip it into kosher salt.
Pour tequila and Cointreau into the glass with ice.
Fill it with sweet and sour mix or margarita mix.
Add the float of Grand Marnier on top

~ Lynchburg Lemonade
> 2 oz Jack Daniels
> 1/2 oz Triple Sec
> Sweet & Sour Mix
> Lemon-Lime Soda
Pour the Jack Daniels and triple sec into a glass with ice. Fill it with equal parts of sweet and sour and lemon-lime soda.

~Orgasm
> 1/2 oz vodka
> 1/2 oz Triple Sec
> 1/2 oz Amaretto
> 1/2 oz Creme de Cacao
1/2 oz Cream / Half & Half
Pour all ingredients into a shaker with ice. Shake and strain into a chilled martini glass.

~Three Wise Men
> 1/2 oz Jack Daniels
> 1/2 oz Jim Beam
> 1/2 oz Johnnie Walker
Pour all ingredients into a shaker with ice. Shake and strain into a shot glass.

~Fuzzy Navel
> 2 oz Peach Schnapps
> Orange Juice
Combine ingredients, its easy

~B-52
> 1/2 oz Coffee Liqueur
> 1/2 oz Irish Cream
> 1/2 oz Grand Marnier
Pour in layers the coffee liqueur then the Irish cream and finally the Grand Marnier.

~ Surfer on Acid
> 1 oz Jagermeister
> 1 oz Coconut Rum
> 1/2 oz Pineapple Juice
Pour all ingredients into a shaker with ice. Shake and strain into a shot glass

~ Scooby Snack
> 1 oz Melon Liqueur
> 1 oz Coconut Rum
> 1/2 oz Pineapple Juice
> 1/2 oz Cream/Half & Half

~ Bloody Mary
> 2 oz Vodka
> 3 drops Worcestershire Sauce
